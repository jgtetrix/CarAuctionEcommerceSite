Who are we?

BidNDrive is an online auction site that specializes in selling used cars. With BidNDrive, users can browse a large selection of vehicles and place bids on the ones that they are interested in purchasing. The site is designed to be user-friendly, with a simple and intuitive interface that allows users to easily navigate through the available cars and bidding process.

Our Goal is for you to leave this website with your dream car purchased and and ready to drive.

How does it work?

Browse through our collection of new and used cars currently listed for auction and find the car that meets your qualifications.



Place a bid on the car of your choosing. Keep checking on it until the time ends to keep your top bid.



If your bid is the highest when the listing ends you will recieve an email with your order confirmation. Schedule a pick-up or drop off and enjoy.
